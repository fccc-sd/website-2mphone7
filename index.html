My Early Life

I was born in Bryan, Ohio. I grew up going to Bryan Elementary Schools. A fun little story, I said almost absolutely nothing during school classes, and so the teachers assumed I was deaf. This became an issue so much that they actually sent me to a doctor to check on me, only to find out I just didn't understand what anyone was saying. I was still learning English, so they sent me to special classes. I don't really remember much from going to Bryan. Eventually, my parents could not bear living with each other. So my mom and I went to Salt Lake City, Utah where she went to live with her sister. I lived in Utah for about 5 years, but despite living there, I rarely went outside at all. I regret not going outside, biking, and enjoying the neighborhood. School and games were the only thing that kept me going in life. My mom later decides to move to North Carolina, but I only stayed there for about 3 months, before finally moving back to live with my dad here in Ohio. Even though Montpelier was a small village, I enjoyed being there more than being at NC. It was smaller, peaceful, but still had enough livelihood around it.
